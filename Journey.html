My Journey

From curiosity to purpose — my journey has been one of constant exploration, learning, and transformation. I’ve always believed that knowledge should not just inform, but also heal, protect, and empower. Over the years, I’ve learned to weave science and technology together to create meaningful impact — and this pursuit continues to shape who I am today.

Origin & Early Interests

2010 - 2018

Even as a student, I was deeply fascinated by how data could reveal hidden patterns in life itself. My curiosity began with biology — understanding the complexity of living systems — and soon grew into a desire to decode it through technology. This blend of curiosity and logic drew me to bioinformatics, where science meets computation to uncover the unseen. It marked the beginning of my lifelong fascination with merging biological insight and digital innovation.

Higher Education & Skill Development

2019 - 2025

Pursuing my Bachelor’s in Bioinformatics, Data Science, and Statistics provided me with the foundation to understand both the theoretical and practical sides of data-driven science. Through projects, research, and coding experience, I developed proficiency in Python, R, and analytical tools for genomic research. My work in breast cancer bioinformatics strengthened not only my technical skills but also my sense of purpose — using data as a tool for discovery and restoration. Alongside this, my interest in cybersecurity grew as I realized the importance of safeguarding digital information and scientific data in a world driven by technology.

Professional Experience & Current Vision

2025 - Present

Today, I continue to expand my expertise through independent research, collaborative projects, and continuous learning. I am currently focused on AI-driven medical research and preparing to pursue a Master’s in Cybersecurity, where I aim to bridge scientific integrity with digital safety. My vision is to contribute to a world where technology doesn’t just advance intelligence — it preserves truth, privacy, and life itself.
